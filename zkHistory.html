Zeta Kappa History

The Mighty Zeta Kappa Chapter of Omega Psi Phi Fraternity Inc. was chartered on the campus of The University of South Alabama on May 10,1974. Since the chartering, The Zeta Kappa chapter has been upholding the cardinal principles: Manhood, Scholarship, Perservearance, and Uplift
